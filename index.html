About This Project

The DIU Question Bank is a student-led initiative aimed at creating a centralized, accessible, and free archive of past examination questions. We believe that by providing these resources, we can help fellow students better understand exam patterns, prepare more effectively, and achieve academic excellence.

  • For Students, By Students: A community-driven platform for academic support.
  • Always Free: We are committed to keeping this resource open and accessible to all DIU students.
  • Regular Updates: New question papers are added continuously with each semester.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the DIU Question Bank.

How current are the question papers?

We strive to update our collection after each semester. Most departments have questions from the last 3-5 years, with newer departments having questions from their inception.

Are solutions provided with the questions?

Currently, we primarily provide question papers. Some departments have select papers with solutions contributed by faculty and students. We're working to add more solutions in the future.

How can I request a specific question paper?

You can use the contribution form to request specific papers. While we can't guarantee immediate availability, we prioritize requested papers in our collection efforts.

Help Us Grow!

Have a question paper that's not in our bank? Your contribution can help thousands of other students. Share your resources and become a part of our collaborative community.
